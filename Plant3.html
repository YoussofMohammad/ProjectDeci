Snake plant (Sansevieria)

About Snake Plant

The Snake Plant, also known as Sansevieria or Mother-in-Law's Tongue, is a hardy houseplant recognized for its upright, sword-like leaves. It's an excellent choice for beginners due to its low maintenance needs and ability to purify indoor air.

Care Tips:

  • Light: Tolerates a wide range of light conditions, from low light to bright, indirect sunlight.
  • Water: Allow the soil to dry out completely between waterings; overwatering can cause root rot.
