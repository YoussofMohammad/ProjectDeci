Dracaena

About Dracaena

Dracaena is a popular houseplant known for its elegant, sword-like leaves and varied colors. It’s valued for its hardy nature and ability to add a touch of tropical flair to indoor spaces.

Care Tips:

  • Light: Prefers bright, indirect light; can tolerate lower light but grows more slowly.
  • Water: Allow the top inch of soil to dry out between waterings; avoid overwatering.
  • Temperature: Best in temperatures between 65-80°F (18-27°C).
  • Fertilizer: Use a balanced fertilizer every 1-2 months during the growing season for optimal growth.
