Lucky bamboo (Dracaena sanderiana)

About Lucky Bamboo

Lucky Bamboo, despite its name, is not a true bamboo but a resilient plant known for its ease of care and symbolic representation of good fortune and prosperity. It’s often used in Feng Shui for its decorative appeal and low-maintenance needs.

Care Tips:

  • Light: Prefers bright, indirect light; can tolerate low light.
  • Water: Keep roots submerged in water; change water every 2 weeks.
  • Temperature: Best in temperatures between 65-85°F (18-29°C).
  • Fertilizer: Feed with a liquid houseplant fertilizer every 1-2 months.
