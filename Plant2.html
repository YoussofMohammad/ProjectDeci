Peace lily (Spathiphyllum)

About Peace Lily

The Peace Lily is a popular houseplant admired for its elegant white blooms and glossy green leaves. It's known for its air-purifying qualities and is relatively easy to care for, making it a favorite among plant enthusiasts.

Care Tips:

  • Light: Thrives in low to medium indirect light but can tolerate lower light conditions.
  • Water: Keep the soil consistently moist but not waterlogged; water when the top inch of soil is dry.
  • Temperature: Prefers temperatures between 65-80°F (18-27°C).
  • Fertilizer: Feed every 6-8 weeks during the growing season with a balanced, water-soluble fertilizer.
