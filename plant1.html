Pothos (Epipremnum aureum)

About Pothos

Pothos, also known as Devil's Ivy, is a popular houseplant known for its trailing vines and ease of care. It thrives in various lighting conditions and is a great choice for beginners.

Care Tips:

  • Light: Prefers indirect sunlight but can tolerate low light.
  • Water: Allow soil to dry out between waterings.
  • Temperature: Thrives in temperatures between 60-80°F (16-27°C).
  • Fertilizer: Feed monthly during the growing season.
