Spider plant (Chlorophytum comosum)

About Spider Plant

The Spider Plant, known for its arching, striped leaves and spider-like offshoots, is a popular and resilient houseplant. It’s admired for its air-purifying properties and ease of care, making it ideal for both beginners and seasoned plant enthusiasts.

Care Tips:

  • Light: Prefers bright, indirect light but can tolerate low light conditions.
  • Water: Keep soil moderately moist; let top inch dry out.
  • Temperature: Thrives in temperatures between 65-75°F (18-24°C).
  • Fertilizer: Feed monthly during the growing season.
