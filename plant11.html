Clivia

About Clivia

Clivia is a striking houseplant known for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers and lush, evergreen leaves. It's appreciated for its ability to bloom indoors and its relatively low-maintenance care.

Care Tips:

  • Light: Prefers bright, indirect light; can tolerate some direct sunlight.
  • Water: Keep soil evenly moist; let the top inch dry out between waterings.
  • Temperature: Thrives in temperatures between 50-70°F (10-21°C).
  • Fertilizer: Feed with a balanced fertilizer every 1-2 months during the growing season to support blooming.
