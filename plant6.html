Hoya plant

About Hoya Plant

The Hoya, also known as Wax Plant, is a charming houseplant appreciated for its thick, waxy leaves and fragrant, star-shaped flowers. It’s known for its resilience and long-lasting blooms, making it a favorite among plant lovers.

Care Tips:

  • Light: Prefers bright, indirect light; can tolerate some direct sunlight.
  • Water: Allow the soil to dry out between waterings; avoid overwatering.
  • Temperature: Thrives in temperatures between 60-80°F (15-27°C).
  • Fertilizer: Feed with a balanced fertilizer every 1-2 months during the growing season.
